Leumeah Public School

Leumeah Public School is located 3 km northeast of Campbelltown. The school is characterised by diverse family dynamics and cultures which make up its school community and provide a safe and positive environment for our students to learn. At Leumeah Public School we provide a happy, caring environment where learners have diverse and challenging opportunities to develop their potential in all areas of school life. We take pride in developing knowledge, skills, attitudes and values that lead to academic, cultural, social and sporting success. Students are encouraged to develop a love of learning, self-esteem, effective communication and decision-making skills and foster interpersonal relationships of enduring quality. Leumeah Public School's aim is to ensure that all students receive relevant, comprehensive and stimulating educational programs. Quality teaching with experienced and committed staff supports high academic expectations. Emphasis on technology, sporting and environmental programs, enrich the student's learning.
